usage: ssb-tokens [--help] <command> [<args>]

COMMANDS:

    burn        destroy some of your tokens
    create      mint new tokens
    flag        assign a label to tokens
    give        give some of your tokens to someone else
    list        list your tokens or those of others
    trace       trace the history of tokens
    unflag      remove a label you assigned

See 'ssb-tokens help <command>' for help about a specific command.
